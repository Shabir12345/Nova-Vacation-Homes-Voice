Architecture

How the System Works

When a customer calls Nova, the AI takes over instantly — transcribing speech in real time, classifying intent, routing to the right agent, and responding in under a second.

📞
Voice Layer
Twilio ConversationRelay handles the call. Deepgram transcribes speech to text. ElevenLabs converts AI responses back to natural-sounding voice in real time.
🧠
AI Brain
Claude (Anthropic) runs the decision engine. Prompt caching cuts costs ~80% and latency by ~300ms per turn. Three specialized agents handle different call types.
🗄️
Data Layer
PostgreSQL stores all customers, calls, and service requests. Redis manages live call sessions. Your property management system is read directly — no data migration needed.
🔀
Routing Engine
A state machine with 29 distinct call states ensures every conversation follows a precise path — from greeting to booking confirmation or human handoff.
🔔
Notifications
Escalation alerts and intake summaries fire to your Slack channel the moment a caller needs human attention or submits a reservation inquiry.
🛡️
Security & Scale
Twilio signature validation, per-number rate limiting, encrypted data, and Docker containerization. CI/CD pipeline auto-tests every code change.

What We Need From You

To make the AI significantly smarter and to go live with real calls, there are a few key inputs we need from your team. Each one directly improves how the agent handles your specific customers.

The AI learns from your best calls.
The more context you share about how your team currently handles calls, the smarter and more accurate the AI becomes. Here's exactly what would help most:
🎧 Successful Call Examples
Recordings or transcripts of 5–10 calls where your team handled things perfectly — a smooth booking, a service request resolved well, a great escalation. These become training examples for the AI's tone, phrasing, and decision logic.
"Here's exactly how we like to handle a caller asking about pet-friendly properties..."
📋 Your Most-Asked Questions
A list of the 20–30 questions your team answers most on the phone — anything about check-in, parking, pet policies, amenities, cancellations, pricing. We'll load these directly into the AI's knowledge base.
"What time is check-in?" "Is there a hot tub?" "Can I bring my dog?" — these exact phrasing variants matter.
🚫 Edge Cases & Hard No's
Situations where the AI should always escalate to a human — no matter what. Examples: angry guests, security concerns, special event bookings, large group inquiries, accessibility needs. Defining these protects your brand.
"Never let the AI commit to a price override" or "Always escalate groups over 10 people."
🏷️ Pricing & Policy Details
Current cancellation policies, deposit requirements, check-in/check-out times, pet fees, cleaning fees, and any seasonal pricing rules the agent should communicate confidently — or know to avoid committing to.
The AI won't quote prices it doesn't know, but knowing your general ranges prevents "I'm not sure" responses.
🧑‍💼 Team Escalation Contacts
Who does the AI route to for different call types? Is there a different contact for maintenance vs. new bookings vs. owner inquiries? What are your business hours? We need to configure the escalation logic precisely.
Slack channels, phone numbers, or email addresses for each escalation type.
📞 Live Twilio Number
To go live with real calls, we need a Twilio phone number pointed at the agent. This can be your existing business number (via call forwarding) or a new number. Takes 15 minutes to configure once decided.
Decision needed: forward existing number, or provision a new one for testing first?
🎭 Brand Voice Guidelines
Words and phrases your team always uses — or never uses. Your company's personality: warm and casual, or professional and concise? Should the agent introduce itself by name? We can tune the voice to match perfectly.
"We always say 'we'd love to have you' — never 'your request has been logged.'"
🔑 ElevenLabs Voice Selection
The current voice IDs are placeholders. We need you to listen to a few ElevenLabs voice samples and pick the voice that best represents Nova Vacation Homes for English, Spanish, and Portuguese callers.
We can demo 5–6 voice options in a 10-minute call and confirm the final selection.
